Overview

Purpose

Hey!
Here I would like to share my study plan for this semester. In the spring semester, I took a total of 15
credits of courses. But for these courses, I mainly focused on math, algorithms, web basics, and English writing.
About the pre-reading.
The day before each class, I would preview the content of the next day's class, noting down any concepts or steps that I
didn't quite understand. Or if there are any questions about the chapter. If I had friends who had taken the course, I
would talk to them first about what the hardest part of the course was. Gathering more information about the course you have chosen will help you learn better.
About the assignments.
I usually read through the assignment requirements first to estimate a completion time based on my experience.
Roughly think about the general framework of the project and what syntax will be used. If the assignment is
difficult and there are many concepts that I am not familiar with, I will start writing it in advance because
I don't know what problems I will encounter. Once you encounter a problem you can't solve, you can ask the instructor in advance of the class or after the class.
About the review.
Usually, I will divide the course according to its difficulty and how much I think I have learned.
If the chapter of the week I think is not too difficult, I will read some additional content about the chapter.
If there is something I don't understand about the week's chapter that I can't explain to others fluently. I will focus on what I don't understand and try to figure it out. Usually, I don't let what I don't understand be left until next week.

Audience

I think my target customers are students. Maybe seeking how to get a study plan. Maybe through this site, they would know others' study plans and create themself plans. Here is just a framework. It doesn't give details about each course plan. My audience usually accesses my site through an internet link.
